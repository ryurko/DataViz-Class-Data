This data consists of the percentage composition of 8 fatty acids (palmitic, palmitoleic, stearic, oleic, linoleic,linolenic, arachidic, eicosenoic) found in the lipid fraction of 572 Italian olive oils. (An analysis of this data is given in (Forina, Armanino, Lanteri & Tiscornia 1983)). 

There are 9 collection regions, 4 from southern Italy
(North and South Apulia, Calabria, Sicily), 3 from northern Italy (Umbria, East and West Liguria) and 2 from Sardinia (Inland and Coastal).
The data available are:

Areas: South, Sardinia or North

Sub-regions within the larger areas: (North and South Apulia,
Calabria, Sicily, Inland and Coastal Sardinia, Umbria, East and
West Liguria
